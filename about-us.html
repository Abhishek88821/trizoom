About Trizoom

Trizoom Solutions is a leading provider of enterprise communication and customer experience management solutions. With profound expertise in providing state-of-the-art communication solutions for organizations across industry verticals. The team at Trizoom is experienced to consult, design, implement, train and support enterprise voice, video, unified communication, data networking, security and customer experience management solutions.

Trizoom Solutions brings audio visual products and solutions that seamlessly integrates audio, video, display, lighting equipment and system controls for applications such as Boardrooms, Conference Rooms, Auditoriums, Training Rooms, Cafeterias, Telepresence Rooms and much more. Our partnership with leading audio visual product manufacturers across the world, enable us to offer state-of-the-art products and solutions.

Our expertise includes all kind of AV infrastructure solutions like MCU, Gatekeeper, Provisioning Server, Firewall Traversal, Recording & Streaming, cloud based audio & video for both meeting room and mobility. Our people are certified from industry leaders like Polycom, Cisco, Avaya, Huawei, Lifesize, Crestron, Extron, AMX, Logitech, Jabra, Grandstream, BenQ, Epson, SONY, Samsung, LG, Panasonic, etc.

We deal into all kinds of IT solutions & services like switches, routers, firewall, servers, storage, laptops, desktops, printers, security & surveillance systems, etc.

Since its establishment, Trizoom focuses on Customer Service through dedicated team of result-oriented and qualified telecom professionals with ability to understand and resolve specific technical problems and provide creative, practical and economically viable solutions to business related issues. We assure our customers industry best services always.

If you need help in slecting the right product and solution, call us today. We provide free consultancy.
