Data Networking, Security & Wi-fi

A business network enables everyone in your company to connect to each other, to customers, to business partners, to important applications and to the Internet. The beauty of a network is that information and applications can be shared and accessed easily by everyone on your team.

Trizoom provides Turnkey Network Solutions including structured cabling, Wi Fi, Routing, Switching, LAN and WAN technologies that fits to your business requirement. We have wide range of Wi Fi solution to cover your complete building and support application for data, voice and video.

We have variety of active & security products rages from Cisco, Juniper, Avaya, HP, Grandstream etc. and we are expert in designing and implementing network (wired and wireless) with complete security for application voice, video and data.

Trizoom Solutions is a leading provider of enterprise communication and customer experience management solutions. With profound expertise in providing state-of-the-art communication solutions for organizations across industry verticals.
